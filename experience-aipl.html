About:

Automotive Infotronics Private Limited was a Joint Venture of Continental AG and Ashok Leyland Group. Ashok Leyland is one of the world’s largest truck and bus manufacturer. Instrument Panel Cluster, Body Control unit and Telematics are some of the Products. Automotive Infotronics Private Limited was later split and merged with Ashok Leyland and ContinentalAG.

Was part of the team that developed software for Telematics, Navigation & Passenger Information System

General Responsibilities:

  • Part of a small Embedded team that operated on the Waterfall model.

  • Responsible for Embedded Software Development such as Drivers, Middleware and Application Development in Microcontrollers and Microprocessors.

  • Responsible for Requirement Analysis, perform low level Design, Implementation and Unit & Functional Testing.

  • Participated in Design and peer Code reviews. Supported verification team on Automated test scripts.

  • Supported Board Bringup, Porting of U-Boot, Kernel and Rootfs.

  • Experience in working from Scratch development to stable release and deployment.
